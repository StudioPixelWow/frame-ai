## 2. Hebrew Copywriting Best Practices

### 2.1 Tone and Voice for Israeli Audiences

Israelis respond to direct, no-nonsense communication. The "dugri" (דוגרי) culture values straight talk.

```
DO:
  ✓ Be direct — state the value proposition in the first sentence
  ✓ Use "אתה/את" (informal you) even in B2B — Israeli business culture is informal
  ✓ Numbers and specifics: "חסוך 30% בעלויות שיווק" beats "חסוך כסף"
  ✓ Social proof — Israeli businesses trust peer recommendations highly
  ✓ Bullet points — Israelis skim, not read
  ✓ Humor in B2C — self-deprecating, witty, culturally aware
  ✓ WhatsApp CTA — more trusted than email in Israel

DON'T:
  ✗ Overly formal language (לשון רמה) in most contexts
  ✗ Corporate jargon without meaning
  ✗ Excessive adjectives and superlatives without proof
  ✗ Political/religious content unless you know your audience
  ✗ Use "פתרונות" (solutions) alone — it's overused; be specific
  ✗ Ignore RTL design — text alignment affects readability and trust
```

### 2.2 Hebrew Copywriting Formulas

**AIDA in Hebrew:**
```
תשומת לב (Attention): [Bold claim or question]
עניין (Interest): [Why this matters to the reader]
רצון (Desire): [Specific benefit + social proof]
פעולה (Action): [Clear CTA with urgency if applicable]

Example (for a web design company):
  A: "אתר שלא מביא לידים הוא הוצאה, לא השקעה."
  I: "80% מהלקוחות שלנו מדווחים על עלייה של 40%+ בפניות תוך 90 יום."
  D: "בנינו מעל 200 אתרים לעסקים ישראלים — ואנחנו יודעים מה עובד."
  A: "השאירו פרטים לשיחת ייעוץ חינם ב-24 שעות הקרובות."
```

**PAS (Problem-Agitate-Solution) for Israeli Market:**
```
בעיה: "מרגישים שהשיווק שלכם לא עובד?"
החרפה: "כל יום בלי לידים הוא יום שהמתחרים שלכם גדלים."
פתרון: "אנחנו בונים מערכת שיווק שעובדת בשבילכם — 24/7."
```

### 2.3 CTAs That Work in Hebrew

| Context | CTA Text | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Lead gen | "השאר פרטים ונחזור אליך" | Most common and trusted |
| Phone | "חייג עכשיו" / "התקשר" | High-intent |
| WhatsApp | "שלח הודעה בוואטסאפ" | Highest CTR in Israel |
| Free consultation | "לייעוץ חינם ←" | Arrow points LEFT in RTL |
| Download | "הורד עכשיו" / "קבל גישה חינם" | |
| Urgency | "עד [date] בלבד" | Works well pre-holidays |
| Price | "לקבלת הצעת מחיר" | B2B standard |

## 3. B2B vs B2C Israeli Content Strategy

### 3.1 B2B Israeli Content

**Primary audience:** Startup founders, SMB owners, finance managers, marketing managers
**Key platforms:** LinkedIn, Email, WhatsApp (business groups)
**Content that performs:**
- Case studies with measurable ROI (numbers-first)
- Industry reports and data (Israelis are data-driven)
- How-to guides for business operations
- Regulatory/compliance updates (tax, GDPR/Israeli privacy law)
- LinkedIn thought leadership posts from founders/CEOs

**Israeli B2B buying cycle:**
```
Awareness → Research → Demo/consultation → Reference check (call a peer) → Decision
  ^                                              ^
  WhatsApp referral is huge here         Israeli buyers ALWAYS call references
```

### 3.2 B2C Israeli Content

**Primary audience:** 25–45 age group, mobile-first, WhatsApp-native
**Key platforms:** Instagram, TikTok, Facebook groups, WhatsApp
**Content that performs:**
- Before/after visuals
- User-generated content (testimonials via WhatsApp screenshots)
- Video content in Hebrew (short, fast-paced)
- Holiday promotions with countdown
- Local community content ("ברמת גן" / "בתל אביב")

## 4. Blog Post Templates in Hebrew

### 4.1 "מדריך מלא ל..." (Complete Guide) Template

```markdown
# מדריך מלא ל[TOPIC] בישראל — [Year]

**זמן קריאה:** X דקות
**עודכן לאחרונה:** [Date]
**מאת:** [Author Name], [Credentials]

## מה תלמדו במדריך הזה?
- [Point 1]
- [Point 2]
- [Point 3]

---

## מה זה [TOPIC]?
[2-3 paragraph definition, encyclopedic tone for GEO]

## למה [TOPIC] חשוב לעסקים בישראל?
[Israeli market context, statistics if available]

## איך [TOPIC] עובד — שלב אחר שלב
### שלב 1: [Step name]
### שלב 2: [Step name]
### שלב 3: [Step name]

## טעויות נפוצות ב[TOPIC] שכדאי להימנע מהן
[3-5 common mistakes, Israeli-specific where possible]

## כמה עולה [TOPIC] בישראל?
[Pricing table or range — Israelis always want to know the cost]

## שאלות נפוצות
**ש: [Common question]**
ת: [Answer]

## סיכום
[Brief summary + CTA]

---
*רוצים לדעת עוד? [פנו אלינו לייעוץ חינם](/contact)*
```

## 5. Content Distribution Channels — Israel

| Channel | Reach Type | Best For | Notes |
|---|---|---|---|
| WhatsApp Broadcast | High-intent existing customers | Retention, upsell | Need opt-in; 98%+ open rate |
| Instagram | B2C visual content | Awareness, brand | Stories + Reels dominate |
| Facebook Groups | Community building | Local B2C, niche B2B | Very active in Israel |

| YouTube | How-to, demos | B2B and B2C | Hebrew content underserved |

## 6. WhatsApp Marketing Integration

### 6.1 WhatsApp Business Setup

```
WhatsApp Business Account Setup:
  1. Israeli business phone number (not personal)
  2. Business profile: name, description, website, hours
  3. Catalog: products/services with prices in ₪
  4. Auto-reply: immediate response to new messages
  5. Labels: organize contacts by stage (ליד, לקוח, VIP, לא רלוונטי)

Broadcast List Rules (legal & best practice):
  - Recipients must have your number saved
  - Max 256 per broadcast
  - Use WhatsApp Business API for larger lists
  - Always include opt-out option: "להסרה מהרשימה — הגב 'הסר'"
```
