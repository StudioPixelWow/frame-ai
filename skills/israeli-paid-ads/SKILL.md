## 2. Google Ads — Hebrew Keyword Strategy

### 2.1 Hebrew Keyword Match Types

Hebrew behaves differently from English in Google Ads match types due to morphology.

```
Broad Match (התאמה רחבה):
  - Matches all morphological forms automatically
  - Risky in Hebrew — too broad, wastes budget
  - Use only with aggressive negative keywords
  - Example: [קידום אתרים] → may trigger "קידום מכירות"

Phrase Match (התאמה לביטוי):
  - Recommended as primary match type for Hebrew
  - Matches keyword within larger queries
  - Preserves word order
  - Example: "פיתוח אתרים" → triggers "פיתוח אתרים מקצועי בתל אביב"

Exact Match (התאמה מדויקת):
  - Use for highest-intent, highest-value keywords
  - In Hebrew, Google now considers morphological variants as "same intent"
  - Example: [עורך דין תל אביב] may also match [עורכי דין בתל אביב]

Negative Keywords (מילות מפתח שליליות):
  CRITICAL in Hebrew — must add extensive negatives
  Common negatives to add across all campaigns:
    - חינם, בחינם, ללא תשלום (free)
    - קורס, לימוד, הכשרה (if not education client)
    - עבודה, דרושים, משרה (job searches)
    - ויקיפדיה, מה זה, הגדרה (informational)
    - DIY keywords specific to industry
```

### 2.2 Hebrew Ad Copy Structure

```
Responsive Search Ad (RSA) — Hebrew:
  
Headlines (עד 30 תווים כל אחד — Hebrew chars count differently):
  Target: 20-25 Hebrew characters per headline
  
  H1 (Primary keyword): "קידום אתרים בגוגל"
  H2 (USP): "10 שנות ניסיון — תוצאות מוכחות"
  H3 (CTA): "פנה עכשיו לייעוץ חינם"
  H4 (Local signal): "משרד בתל אביב — שירות ארצי"
  H5 (Trust): "500+ לקוחות מרוצים"
  H6 (Offer): "חודש ראשון ב-50% הנחה"

Descriptions (עד 90 תווים):
  D1: "אנחנו מקדמים עסקים ישראלים בגוגל מאז 2015. תוצאות אמיתיות, ללא הבטחות שווא."
  D2: "שירות מלא: בניית אתר, SEO, פרסום בגוגל. צרו קשר לייעוץ חינם עוד היום."

RTL Ad Copy Rules:
  - Always preview ad in Hebrew to check display
  - Numbers (1, 2, 3) display LTR within RTL text — test carefully
  - Avoid mixing Hebrew and English in same headline if possible
  - Emojis: use sparingly, right side of text
```

### 2.3 Campaign Structure for Israeli Market

```
Campaign Structure (SKAG-hybrid for Hebrew):
  
  Campaign: [Service] — Israel
    Ad Group 1: Brand terms (branded keywords)
    Ad Group 2: Service + "בתל אביב" (local intent)
    Ad Group 3: Service + "מחיר/עלות" (commercial intent)
    Ad Group 4: Service + "מקצועי/איכותי" (quality intent)
    Ad Group 5: Competitor terms (if strategy permits)
    Ad Group 6: Problem-aware terms ("פתרון ל...", "עזרה ב...")

  Separate campaigns for:
    - Brand (always separate, usually lowest CPC)
    - Non-brand
    - Remarketing/RLSA
    - Display (if applicable)
```

## 3. Meta Ads — Hebrew RTL Creative Requirements

### 3.1 RTL Creative Specifications

```
Image/Video Ads:
  - Text overlay must be RTL-aligned
  - CTA button text in Hebrew (custom via "Create Custom" button text)
    Available Hebrew CTA buttons: "למידע נוסף", "הגש בקשה", "צור קשר"
    Note: Facebook has limited Hebrew CTA options — use them all
  - Logo placement: RIGHT side of image for Hebrew audiences (natural eye flow)
  - Reading direction: Design information flow right-to-left

Video Ads in Hebrew:
  - Hook in first 3 seconds — Hebrew text on screen, not just voiceover
  - Subtitles mandatory (most Israeli Facebook users watch with sound off)
  - Captions must be RTL and sized for mobile (minimum 18px equivalent)
  - End card: Hebrew CTA + phone number + logo

Carousel Ads (RTL):
  - Cards read LEFT to RIGHT in Facebook's UI
  - But Hebrew story should flow right to left
  - Solution: First card = conclusion/CTA, middle = benefits, last card = problem
  - This is counterintuitive — design deliberately
```

### 3.2 Meta Audience Targeting — Israel

```
Geographic:
  - Israel (full country) — most campaigns start here
  - Specific cities: Tel Aviv, Haifa, Jerusalem, etc.
  - Radius targeting: around specific addresses (useful for local services)
  - Exclude areas if needed (e.g., exclude settlements for some brands)

Language:
  - Hebrew (עברית) — primary
  - Russian — significant population (~1M Russian speakers in Israel)
  - Arabic — Arab Israeli population (~20% of country)
  - English — expats, international companies, some business professionals

Custom Audiences (most powerful):
  - Customer list upload (Israeli phone numbers: 05X format, or emails)
  - Website visitors (pixel-based)
  - Video viewers (30s, 50%, 75%)
  - Instagram/Facebook engagers
  - WhatsApp contacts (via CAPI if WhatsApp Business API connected)

Lookalike Audiences:
  - 1% lookalike from customer list — best quality, smallest size
  - 1-3% for scale
  - Israeli market is small — 1% LAL ≈ ~60,000 people
  - 5-10% LAL for awareness campaigns
```

## 4. TikTok Ads — Israel

### 4.1 TikTok Ad Setup for Israel

```
Campaign objectives available in Israel:
  - Reach (מודעות)
  - Video views
  - Traffic (כניסות לאתר)
  - App installs
  - Conversions (המרות)
  - Lead generation

TikTok Israel targeting:
  - Country: Israel
  - Language: Hebrew
  - Age: 18+ (required), most relevant 18–34
  - Interest categories (Hebrew content interest)
  - Device: iOS vs Android (iOS users skew higher income in Israel)
```

### 4.2 TikTok Creative for Israeli Market

```
Hook formats that work in Israel:
  - Text on screen in first frame: "[X] שלא ידעת על [TOPIC]"
  - Shocking statistic: "90% מהעסקים בישראל טועים ב..."
  - Before/after reveal teaser
  - "אני הולך לספר לכם משהו שרוב אנשי השיווק מסתירים"

Audio strategy:
  - Hebrew trending audio (check TikTok Creative Center)
  - Original voice in Hebrew builds more trust
  - Avoid English music with Hebrew text (disconnected feel)

TikTok Ad Specs (Israel):
  - Ratio: 9:16 vertical preferred
  - Duration: 15–60 seconds (15-30s best for paid)
  - Format: .mp4 or .mov
  - Resolution: 720p minimum, 1080p recommended
```

## 5. LinkedIn Ads — Israeli B2B Targeting

### 5.1 Israeli B2B Audience Targeting on LinkedIn

```
Job Title Targeting (Hebrew and English — use both):
  C-Suite: "מנכ"ל", "CEO", "CTO", "CFO", "CMO"
  Directors: "מנהל שיווק", "מנהל מכירות", "VP Marketing"
  Managers: "מנהל פרויקטים", "Product Manager"

Company targeting:
  - Israeli tech companies (use company list upload)
  - Company size: 50-200 (SMB sweet spot in Israel)
  - Industry: Hi-Tech, Finance, Healthcare, Legal, Real Estate

LinkedIn Israel campaign types:
  1. Sponsored Content — best for awareness + leads
  2. Message Ads (InMail) — high-intent, expensive, use sparingly
  3. Lead Gen Forms — best conversion rate for B2B in Israel
  4. Text Ads — low cost, low click-through, retargeting only

Israeli LinkedIn insight:
  - Post in English for tech/startup audience
  - Post in Hebrew for traditional industries (legal, finance, real estate, medical)
  - Bilingual posts (Hebrew intro, English continuation) get best of both worlds
```

## 7. Israeli Advertising Regulations

### 7.1 חוק הגנת הצרכן (Consumer Protection Law)

Key requirements for digital advertising in Israel:

```
Disclosure Requirements:
  - Paid content / influencer marketing: must disclose "#פרסום" or "#שיתוף_פעולה"
  - Sponsored posts: clearly marked as "ממומן" or "פרסומת"
  - Price claims: must show full price including VAT (מע"מ)
  - "From price" (החל מ-): must be genuinely achievable
  - Free trials: exact terms of what becomes paid must be stated

Prohibited Practices:
  - Misleading claims (מצג שווא) — heavily enforced
  - Fake reviews or testimonials
  - Countdown timers that reset (fake urgency)
  - Hidden subscription terms
  - Drip pricing (adding fees at checkout)

Retargeting Data Rules:
  - Must comply with Israeli Privacy Protection Law (חוק הגנת הפרטיות)
  - Cookie consent required (similar to GDPR)
  - Israeli Privacy Authority (הרשות להגנת הפרטיות) oversight
  - Data must not be transferred outside Israel without compliance measures

Medical/Legal/Financial Advertising:
  - Medical services: must be licensed practitioner advertising
  - Dietary supplements: cannot make health claims
  - Financial products: must include risk disclosures
  - Legal services: cannot guarantee outcomes
```

## 8. Budget Allocation Framework — Israeli Clients

### 8.1 Budget by Business Stage

```
Early Stage (₪3,000–8,000/month total):
  60% Google Search (highest intent)
  30% Meta (brand building + retargeting)
  10% Test budget (TikTok or LinkedIn)

Growth Stage (₪8,000–25,000/month):
  45% Google Search
  35% Meta
  10% LinkedIn (if B2B)
  10% TikTok / YouTube

Scale Stage (₪25,000–100,000+/month):
  35% Google (Search + Shopping/Display)
  30% Meta
  15% LinkedIn (B2B) or TikTok (B2C)
  10% YouTube
  10% Programmatic / other

Agency Fee: Typically 15-20% of media spend in Israel,
  or ₪3,500–12,000/month flat management fee
```
